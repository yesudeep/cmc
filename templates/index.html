In India, we begin our day with the aroma of fresh chai. Maybe with a dash of masala for the zing. We greet with a chai. We meet. We bond. We share our everyday triumphs, joys, sorrows, problems. We share stories over that cup of chai. From the rich guy's pot-tea to the common man's "cutting", there is a story in every cuppa chai. Cutting Masala Chai is just the right blend of emotions with a pinch of some soul-stirring spice.

There is a person, a heart, a magic ingredient in every story. Let's make a difference and connect it to the world to offer help; encouragement; resources, whatever it is that Cutting Masala Chai can facilitate.

Cutting Masala Chai is a collection of true enlivening stories. Real stories of real people; people like us who strive or have struggled to win in life with absolute grit and determination. Struggle for themselves and for others. About how we overcome our karmic struggle and beat destiny. Be it career challenges, failing relationships, dwindling finances, illnesses—no hurdle goes unconquered. No obstacle left in peace. That's the indomitable Indian spirit.

Cutting Masala Chai is a humble attempt to salute this thriving Indian spirit. Exhorting stories from the billion lives of India to encourage you and yours, and people around the world. Ingenious inspiration in indigenous style.
